### Types of Philosophy
* Ethical / moral philosophy 
  * analysis of behaviours and actions
  * what is good or bad and what do these words mean?
* Epistemology
  * theory of knowledge
  * what are knowledge and belief
  * how do we obtain them
  * what does it mean for them to be true or false
* Ontology / metaphysics
  * what it means to be
  * how things come to be and cease to be
  * precisely what "is" or exists
  * what reality itself ks
