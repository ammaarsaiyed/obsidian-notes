## Sayyid Muhammad bin Alawi's encounter with Ghawth al Adham

Sayyid Muhammad bin Alawi had lost power in his right hand in his youth.
They had been to many doctors in the east and the west but couldn't cure the issue in his hand.
This is very difficult for a person from a scholarly background and of that stature

He went to the ashab ul badr - for ziyarah, dua, went to Madina al Munawwarah, presented salams - and he was given isharah to travel to baghdad to Shaykh Abdul Qadir al Jilani - and that he would receive the cure there

At this point however, there was war in Iraq which made it difficult to travel - so he made the intention that he would travel in secret - without telling any family members

He planned to travel, go do ziyarah and return without anybody knowing

The day of the departure, he was about to leave home, his honourable wife - Sayyidah Umm Ahmad - came and said to him that she had a dream that he was leaving - and travelling to Baghdad

She says, I had a dream and in my dream, a young man gave me a card, and when he gave me the card he said to me this is an inviation from Shaykh Abdul Qadir al Jilani for Sayyid Muhammad al Makki - give this to him

On the card, it said the name Tariq - and that he would be the Sayyid's host  - she was already given this basharah

The shaykh left for Baghdad, but when he arrived in Baghdad, he entered the mazaar of Shaykh Abdul Qadir al Jilani - but there were a lot of people and a big rush so he was unable to enter

As he was waiting to enter, a man comes to him and says to him - come with me, I will look after you, we will return later when there is less of a rush

The man takes the Shaykh to the hotel - the Shaykh mentioned he didn't ask who the man was nor did the man ask who he was - they just carried on

The next day he took him for ziyarah of Shaykh Abdul Qadir al Jilani - he did ziyarah, made his pledges, and then had breakfast - after which they were to leave for the airport

At this point - he asks the man what is name is, and he says Tariq

He says, I am a soldier from Mosul - I had a dream where Shaykh Abdul Qadir al Jilani came to me and said to me my guest from Makkah is coming so you will host him

After this they parted ways - and after this visist - the feeling came back into the hand of Shaykh Muhammad
