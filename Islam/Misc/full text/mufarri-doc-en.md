## The First Fortress of Relief from Sorrows and Joy for the Hearts

  
(1) In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful * There is no power and no strength except with Allah, the Most High, the Almighty
 
(2) O Allah, I ask of You, for to You belongs all praise; there is no god but You, the Creator of the heavens and the earth, O Possessor of Majesty and Honour, O Ever-Living, O Self-Subsisting

(3) O Allah, I ask of You, for to You belongs all praise; there is no god but You, the Most Merciful, the Most Gracious, the Creator of the heavens and the earth, O Possessor of Majesty and Honour; I ask of You Paradise and seek refuge in You from the Fire  

(4) O Allah, I ask of You—there is no god but You, Lord of the seven heavens and Lord of the Great Throne; I ask of You—there is no god but You, Lord of the seven heavens and Lord of the Noble Throne; I ask of You—there is no god but You, Lord of the seven heavens and the seven earths and all that is therein, for You are All-Powerful over all things (then he should ask for his need)
  
(5) O Lord, O Lord, O Lord

(6) O Creator of the heavens, O Ever-Living, O Self-Subsisting

(7) O Allah, I ask You for all good, that which I know of and that which I do not know, and I ask You by Your Great Name, by which, when called upon, You answer, and when asked, You grant. There is no god but Allah, and Allah is the Greatest. There is no god but Allah alone, with no partner. To Him belongs the Kingdom, and to Him belongs all praise, and He is All-Powerful over all things. There is no god but Allah, and there is no power or strength except with Allah

(8) There is no god but Allah, and Allah is the Greatest; there is no god but Allah alone, with no partner or associate; to Him belongs sovereignty and to Him belongs all praise, and He has power over all things; there is no god but Allah, and there is no power or strength except in Allah.

(9) O Allah, I am Your servant, the son of Your servant, the son of Your female servant; my forelock is in Your hand; Your decree is binding; Your judgement is just. I ask You by every name that belongs to You—whether You have named Yourself by it, revealed it in Your Book, taught it to any of Your creation, or kept it hidden in Your knowledge of the unseen—that You make the Qur’an the spring of my heart, the light of my sight, the dispeller of my sorrow, and the remover of my worries

(10) Sufficient for me is the Lord from among the servants; sufficient for me is the Creator from among the created; sufficient for me is the Provider from among those who are provided for; sufficient for me is He who is sufficient for me; sufficient for me is Allah, and He is the Best of Guardians; sufficient for me is Allah; there is no god but He; upon Him I rely, and He is the Lord of the Mighty Throne

(11) I place my trust in the Living One who never dies. Praise be to Allah, who has not taken a son, nor has He any partner in His dominion, nor has He any protector from humiliation, and I magnify His greatness.

(12) Allah is sufficient for me, and He is the Best of Guardians.

(13) We put our trust in God; He is the best of guardians.

(14) Allah is sufficient for me in such-and-such matters; Allah is sufficient for me in what concerns me; Allah is sufficient against those who wrong me; Allah is sufficient against those who seek to harm me.

(15) You were and You will be, and You are the Ever-Living, You never die; eyes close and stars fade, yet You are the Ever-Living, the Ever-Present; You have established the cycle of time, and there is no sleep, O Ever-Living, O Ever-Present.

(16) There is no god but Allah, the Great, the Forbearing; there is no god but Allah, Lord of the Great Throne; there is no god but Allah, Lord of the heavens, Lord of the earth, Lord of the Noble Throne.

(17) There is no god but Allah, the Forbearing, the Generous. Glory be to Allah, Lord of the seven heavens and Lord of the Great Throne. There is no god but You, exalted is Your majesty and glorious is Your praise, and there is no god but You.

(18) There is no god but Allah, the Most High, the Forbearing. There is no god but Allah, Lord of the Great Throne. There is no god but Allah, Lord of the seven heavens and Lord of the Noble Throne.

(19) Oh Allah! Only to You do I complain of my weakness, my lack of resources, and my lack of respect in the eyes of people. Oh Most Merciful of all those capable of showing mercy. You are the Lord and Caretaker of those who are weak, of those who have been oppressed and wronged—and You are my Lord. Who will you hand me over to, oh Allah? Will you hand me over to a heartless person, who will abuse and mistreat me? Or will You leave me to an enemy who has been given power over me? As long as You are not upset with me, I do not care how these people treat me. Your protection is all that I need. I take refuge in the light and illumination of Your Face, which removes all darkness and solves all of the problems of this life, and the next life. All I need is protection from Your displeasure and Your anger. I will continue to serve you until You are pleased. There is no ability to do good and there is no strength to resist evil—except through You, Oh Allah. All strength comes from You..

(20) O Most Merciful of the merciful (three times)

(21) O Allah, I ask of You and turn to You through Your Prophet Muhammad, peace be upon him, the Prophet of Mercy. (O our Master) O Muhammad, I turn to my Lord through you in my need, that He may grant it. * O Allah, intercede for him in

(22) O Light of the heavens and the earth, O Sustainer of the heavens and the earth, O Support of the heavens and the earth, O Adornment of the heavens and the earth, O Beauty of the heavens and the earth, O Wonder of the heavens and the earth, O Possessor of Majesty and Honour, O Helper of those who seek help, and the Ultimate Desire of those who desire, and the Reliever of the afflicted, and the Reliever of the distressed, and the Answerer of the cries of those who cry out, and the Answerer of the supplication of the distressed , and Remover of great distress, remove my distress, my anxiety and my gloom, for You see my condition

(23) O Possessor of favour that never ceases and which none but You can count

(24) O He whom none knows save Himself, and O He whose power none can fathom, relieve me *

(25) O Allah, O Witness who is never absent, O Near One who is never far, O Victorious One who is never defeated, grant me relief and a way out of my affairs, and provide for me from where I do not expect *

(26) O Allah, I ask You by Your Name, the One and Only, and I call upon You by Your Name, the Self-Subsisting, and I call upon You, O Allah, by Your Great Name, the Odd One, which fills all the corners of the universe, to relieve me of whatever I have been afflicted with, whether at night or in the morning
