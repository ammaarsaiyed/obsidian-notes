	* 39
		* "O Allah! Bless, greet and sanctify our master and liegelord Muħammad, the Tree of Original Light, the Sparkle of the Handful of Divine Mercy, the Best of All Humankind, the Noblest of Physical Frames, the Vessel of the Lord’s Secrets and Storehouse of the Sciences of the Elect, the Possessor of the Original Divine Grasp, Resplendent Grace, and Uppermost Rank, under whose flag line up all the Prophets, so that they are from him and point to him. Bless, greet and sanctify him and his Family and Companions, to the number of all that You have ever created, sustained, caused to die, and caused to live again, to the Day You resurrect those You reduced to dust, and greet him with an abundant and endless greeting. Glory and praise belong to Allah, the Lord of the worlds!"
		* **Benefits**
			* Cause for attaining many lights and the unveiling of many secrets. It is among the greatest means for connecting with the Prophet (peace be upon him) in sleep and wakefulness. It contains secrets for facilitating outward provision (rizq dhahiri), which is the provision for physical bodies (wealth/sustenance), and inward provision (rizq batini), which is the provision for spirits—meaning sciences and divine knowledge. Through it, victory is achieved over the lower self, the devil, and all enemies. It possesses innumerable properties. they mentioned that reciting it three times is equivalent to reading the entirety of Dala'il al-Khayrat. The one reciting it should summon the greatness of the Messenger of Allah (peace be upon him) in his heart, recognizing that he is the greatest means to reaching all good... and a person should not recite it except in a state of ritual purity. Whoever persists in reciting it with these conditions 100 times every day, and continues to do so for forty days with steadfastness, will attain lights and goodness whose magnitude is known only to Allah Almighty. And whoever constantly recites it three times every day after the Dawn (Subh) prayer and three times after the Sunset (Maghrib) prayer will witness many secrets through it, and Allah is the Granter of success.

		* **Benefits**

* fortress one
	* 18: 
		* O Allah, to You I complain of my weakness, my lack of means, and my insignificance before people. You are The Most Merciful of the merciful (Arham ar-Rahimin), You are The Lord (Rabb) of the weak, and You are my Lord. To whom do You entrust me? To a distant stranger who scowls at me, or to an enemy to whom You have given control over my affairs? If You are not angry with me, I do not care, but Your protection is broader for me. I seek refuge in the Light of Your Face, by which darkness is illuminated and the affairs of this world and the Hereafter are set right, lest Your wrath descend upon me or Your anger befall me. To You is the right to reprimand until You are pleased, and there is no power nor strength except by You.  
		* **Context & Benefit:**
			* Du'a of the Prophet at Ta'if after he was rejected and attacked. It is the ultimate prayer of turning to Allah when one feels entirely helpless and abandoned by worldly means.
